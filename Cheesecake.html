Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake Soft Cookies

Lemon Cake

Lemon Blueberry Cheesecake Soft Cookies

Ingredients:

🧀 Cheesecake Filling 🧀

3/4 cup of Cream Cheese
1-1/2 cup of Powdered Sugar
1 drop of Vanilla Essence

🫐 Blueberry Jam 🫐

1-1/2 cup of Blueberries
1-1/2 cup of Powdered Sugar

🍪 Cookie Dough 🍪

1/3 cup of Butter
3/4 cup of Cake Flour
1/3 cup of Powdered Sugar
1 tsp of Baking Powder
1 tsp of Baking Soda
1 tbsp of Lemon Zest
1-1/2 tbsp of a beaten Egg
1 drop of Vanilla Essence
A pinch of Salt

Cheesecake Filling Preparation:

Whisk softened cream cheese with powdered sugar and vanilla essence.

Scoop into 5 small blobs (about 1 tbsp each).

Freeze for 15-20 minutes.

Blueberry Jam Preparation:

Put blueberries and powdered sugar in a pan.

Cook on medium heat, stir occasionally.

Mash slightly and cook until thick (around 10-15 minutes).

Let it cool.

Cookies Preparation:

Combine cake flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a bowl.

In another bowl, rub sugar and lemon zest.

Add butter into sugar and lemon zest bowl, whisk until creamy.

Add egg and vanilla essence and whisk until well combined.

Add the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, mix until just combined.

Fold in blueberry jam (don't overmix).

Divide dough into 5 portions, flatten each piece.

Place frozen cream cheese in center, wrap the dough around it and seal.

Flatten slightly (not too thin).

Place parchment paper inside the air fryer.

Place 2 or 3 cookies on the parchment paper (depending on size).

Bake at 160°C for 5-7 minutes.

Let it cool, and enjoy the cookies!
