Lemon Cake

Lemon Cake

Ingredients:

🍰 Cake 🍰
200g of Spreadable Butter
1 cup of Powdered Sugar
4 Eggs
1-3/4 cups of Wheat Flour
2 tsp of Baking Powder
Zest of 1 Lemon
1-1/2 cup of Buttermilk

🍋 Lemon Syrup 🍋
1/2 cup of Powdered Sugar
1/2 cup of Lemon Juice

🍋 Lemon Icing 🍋
1 cup of Powdered Sugar
3 tsp of Lemon Juice
3 tsp of Milk

Cake Preparation

Combine all the dry ingredients in one bowl. Set aside.

Combine butter and sugar, beat until fluffy (2–3 minutes), then add eggs one by one.

Alternate dry ingredients and buttermilk into the mixture.

Add lemon zest and mix well.

Pour into 14 cm cake tin and bake at 160°C for 35 minutes.

Check doneness with a toothpick.

Lemon Syrup Preparation

Mix sugar and lemon juice until well combined.

Poke holes in cooled cake and pour syrup over it.

Lemon Icing Preparation

Mix sugar, lemon juice, and milk until smooth.

Pour icing over the cake.
